My Senior Design Project (This section is a WIP!)

For my senior design project, my team and I are focused on creating a Blender extension that cleans up motion-captured data to eliminate jitter, assist with rescaling, and deleting repetitive and unnecessary keyframes. We wanted to use Blender since it is free and open source and a wide range of artists use the software.

We've spoken with Chuck Ghislandi, the senior motion capture manager of playstation studios and Em Dang from the SCU Imaginarium to highlight the difficulties working with motion capture. Since, we have little to no experience in the medium, they assisted us greatly by providing us issues we can tackle in out extension.

We are working in Python and learned Blender's extension format.
